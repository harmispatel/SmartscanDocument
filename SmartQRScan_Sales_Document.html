5. Why Choose Smart QR Scan?

For Hotels

  • Less phone congestion — guests order via QR instead of calling reception.
  • Fewer errors — orders go directly to kitchen/POS, no manual writing or mishearing.
  • One QR per room — simple for guests and easy for you to manage.
  • Room service + restaurant + banquet — one system for F&B and events.

For Restaurants

  • Table service — QR on each table; orders go straight to the kitchen.
  • Delivery — define delivery areas and timings in the backend.
  • Take-away — support pickup orders in the same system.
  • Always up-to-date — change menu and prices without reprinting.

For Both

  • Custom theme — colors and look match your brand.
  • Fully responsive — works on all devices.
  • Visitor statistics — see how many people view your menu.
  • Instant updates — add/remove categories and products, change prices, activate/deactivate items by availability.
  • Photos — add images to categories and products for a richer menu.
